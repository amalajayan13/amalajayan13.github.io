About Me

I am Amal Ajayan, a cybersecurity researcher and PhD scholar at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, India, with growing international academic exposure across countries. My research interests include cybersecurity, digital forensics, network security, threat intelligence, cyber defense, and machine learning for security applications. With an academic background in Cyber Forensics and experience in research, publication, peer review, and mentoring, I aim to contribute to globally relevant, interdisciplinary, and impact-driven cybersecurity research.

I welcome research collaborations, academic partnerships, and professional opportunities. Please feel free to contact me by email.
